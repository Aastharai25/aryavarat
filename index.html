Who We Are

Aryavart Pre-Primary and Primary School is a nurturing space where children don't just learn—they flourish.

Rooted in values, creativity, and child-centered education, we foster an environment of discovery, exploration, and emotional safety. Our experienced faculty and modern facilities create the perfect foundation for your child's educational journey.

  • CBSE-affiliated curriculum
  • Low student-teacher ratio
  • Safe and secure campus
  • Holistic development focus

Why Choose Aryavart?

Unique Curriculum

Our curriculum blends academic excellence with practical learning—specially designed for young, curious minds. From phonics to foundational science, every lesson builds critical thinking and joyful learning.

Brainstorming Activities

We believe every child is a thinker. Our activity-based learning encourages questioning, collaboration, and creativity through puzzles, experiments, storytelling, and hands-on projects.

All-Round Development

From stage performances to sports days, we ensure each child shines. We focus on holistic growth—emotional, physical, and intellectual—to build confidence and curiosity.
